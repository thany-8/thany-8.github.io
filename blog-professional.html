Learning Through Events and New Connections

I genuinely enjoy attending conferences, networking events, workshops, and other professional gatherings. I like being in spaces where people are open to sharing what they are building, what they have learned, and the challenges they faced along the way.

What I enjoy most is hearing the real story behind someone’s career. Sometimes one honest conversation can teach me more than a long presentation. I like asking questions, learning how other people found their opportunities, and hearing advice from those who were once in the same position I am in now.

For me, networking is not about collecting as many contacts as possible. It is about meeting people I can learn from, staying in touch, and building connections that feel genuine. I usually leave an event feeling more motivated, with a new idea to explore or a different perspective on my own path in technology.
